Germany is one of the top destinations for software engineers relocating from Ireland, India, and beyond.

## Average Salaries

- Junior developer: €45,000 – €55,000/year
- Mid-level: €60,000 – €75,000/year  
- Senior: €80,000 – €100,000/year

## Cost of Living

Berlin is significantly cheaper than Dublin or London. A one-bedroom apartment in Berlin costs around €1,200/month compared to €2,000+ in Dublin.

## Visa Options

The German Job Seeker Visa lets you come to Germany for 6 months to find a job. Once hired, you switch to a work permit.

## Is It Worth Moving?

If you value work-life balance, job security, and European lifestyle, Germany is hard to beat.
